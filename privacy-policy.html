PrivacyEye Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2025

Privacy First: PrivacyEye is designed with your privacy as the top priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or scan data.

1. Introduction

Welcome to PrivacyEye ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how our PrivacyEye mobile application works and our commitment to your privacy.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information from you. PrivacyEye operates completely offline and does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Store scan results or data
  • Transmit any information to our servers
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Require account creation

3. Local Processing Only

All security scans and detections are performed locally on your device:

  • Hidden camera detection runs entirely on your device
  • Network scanning processes data locally
  • Bluetooth scanning operates offline
  • Magnetic field detection works without internet
  • No scan results are saved or transmitted

4. Subscription Information

For premium subscriptions:

  • All payment processing is handled by Apple App Store
  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • Subscription status is verified through Apple's systems
  • No financial data is stored or processed by us

5. Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and payment processing
  • Apple Analytics: Basic crash reporting (can be disabled in device settings)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Data Security

Since we don't collect data, your security is guaranteed by:

  • Complete offline operation
  • No data transmission
  • No server storage
  • Local processing only

7. Your Privacy Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, you have complete control:

  • No data to access, correct, or delete
  • No tracking to opt-out of
  • No accounts to manage
  • Complete anonymity guaranteed

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. Since we don't collect any data, there is no risk of collecting information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Support: support@privacyeye.app

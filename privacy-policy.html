PrivacyEye Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PrivacyEye ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our PrivacyEye mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Account information (if you create an account)
  • Payment information (for premium subscriptions)
  • Support communications

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Our app may collect certain information automatically:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data

2.3 Local Data Processing

Important: PrivacyEye performs all security scans locally on your device. No scan data, images, or detection results are transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Process payments for premium features
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important updates and notifications

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All scan data remains on your device
  • No personal data is transmitted during security scans
  • Encrypted data transmission for account-related information
  • Regular security updates and patches

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Payment processing (Apple App Store)
  • Analytics and crash reporting
  • Cloud storage (if you choose to sync data)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your data

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Users

If you are using our app from outside your country of residence, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries with different privacy laws.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
